About Christine Teigen

Christine Diane Teigen is an American model, television personality and author who made her debut in the annual Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue in 2010 and appeared on the cover in 2014. Since 2015, she co-hosts Lip Sync Battle with LL Cool J on Paramount Network. Christine is married to John Legend and is also a mother to her two kids: Luna and Miles.
